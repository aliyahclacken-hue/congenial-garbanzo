How To Play

Touch exploration: drag around the world area. The game speaks what you touch. Double tap the world area to activate the selected object. Double tap activates the selected object. The main Activate button has been removed.

Sounds unlock after your first tap. If iPhone blocks a sound, the game tries again after interaction.

Children move by age. Newborns stay put, babies crawl, toddlers wander nearby, older children can move around and attend school.

The world keeps progressing while closed. When you continue, it calculates missed time, illness, pregnancy, school, nursery, hospital and money events.

Use the phone, but people can also call and message you randomly.

Illnesses use causes and sound sequences. Older children and adults can approach you and say what is wrong. Use medicine, thermometer, sick bowl, towels, wipes, water, bathroom care and rest. Your character can eat and drink too.
