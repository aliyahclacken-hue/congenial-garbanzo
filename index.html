How To Play

Move by finding doors, paths, stairs, streets, cars and buildings. No Previous Room / Next Room buttons.

Touch exploration works best with VoiceOver off. Drag your finger to hear what is in the place.

Time advances by actions, not constantly. Grandma should no longer run away while you are trying to find her.

Use Listen to hear nearby people, children, pets and useful environmental cues.

At the shop, get a trolley. Babies and toddlers can go in the trolley child seat after you take them out of the car.

Doctors and hospitals can now actually move a child into treatment, triage, admission, discharge and recovery instead of only asking questions.

Adoption children can be touched in the adoption centre as world objects, then adopted from the adoption screen.

Children can move around the home after actions, but time no longer runs constantly while you are searching.
